Features

Island Breeze Apartments is a secure complex comprising of 2 and 3 bedroom apartments all beautifully renovated for your comfort and convenience.

With hillside positioning, all apartments capture ocean breezes and ceiling fans are fitted in each room.

The main bedroom has a queen size bed with access through the large sliding doors
to your own private courtyard, while the second bedroom is fitted with two single beds or can be made up as another double room to suit your needs.

Each apartment has it’s own private courtyard with table and chairs, surrounded by lovely tropical gardens.

The apartments are also fitted out with washing machines and clothes dryers, along with wireless internet and satellite tv.
